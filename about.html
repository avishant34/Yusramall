The Heart of Shopping, Leisure, and Community.

At Yusra Mall, we take pride in bringing you an exceptional variety of stores designed to cater to every lifestyle and preference. From renowned national brands to beloved local favorites, we are Nawada's ultimate destination.

Our Mission

At Yusra Mall, our mission is to provide an exceptional shopping and lifestyle experience for everyone who walks through our doors. We are dedicated to offering a diverse mix of retail stores, dining options, entertainment zones, and leisure experiences—all conveniently located under one roof.

Our goal is to make every visit to Yusra Mall seamless, convenient, and truly memorable, ensuring that each guest leaves with a sense of satisfaction and joy.

We are committed to being the go-to destination for quality shopping, comfort, and entertainment in the community—making Yusra Mall not just a mall, but a place where families and friends of all ages come together to shop, dine, relax, and create lasting memories.

Our Vision

At Yusra Mall, our vision is to be the region's most trusted, innovative, and dynamic shopping and entertainment destination. We strive to redefine the mall experience by setting new standards in customer service and creating a space where everyone feels welcome.

Our vision is to build a vibrant and inclusive environment that not only meets but consistently exceeds the expectations of our visitors. By continuously evolving and expanding, we aim to meet the ever-changing needs of our community, making Yusra Mall a true hub of activity where people can shop, dine, relax, and enjoy life.
